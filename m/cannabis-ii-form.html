II. PREVIOUS AND CURRENT CANNABIS USE
The following questions ask about your experience with cannabis.

Please read each question carefully.

Medicinal cannabis refers to cannabis used to relieve symptoms or medical conditions. This can involve cannabis products PRESCRIBED by a doctor (including THC and/or CBD containing oils, capsules, sprays or plant material) or obtained through NON-PRESCRIBED routes (e.g., from family, friends, a dealer, or purchased online).

Non-medicinal (or recreational) cannabis refers to cannabis use for non-medicinal reasons, e.g., to relax, for pleasure, or to get ‘high’.

Your responses are anonymous and confidential.
