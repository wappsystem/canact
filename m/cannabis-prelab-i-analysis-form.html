Free analysis of your cannabis


You are invited to take part in cannabis sample analysis because you’ve indicated you currently use or grow cannabis.

If you would like to have your personal cannabis sample analysed, you will need to prepare your sample according to the instructions provided on the Free analysis of your cannabis webpage of the CAN-ACT Website.

This process involves a number of simple steps. Please read the instructions carefully, it is designed to ensure that we safely receive and process your sample and can report the results back to you, but that you and your address remain anonymous.

You can then contact the study Courier to arrange sample pickup, free of charge.

All cannabis samples will be analysed for cannabinoid and terpenoid content.
  • The most well-known cannabinoids are tetrahydrocannabinol (THC), responsible for the feeling of being “high”, and cannabidiol (CBD) which is known for antianxiety effects; however, many other cannabinoids exist and may also have therapeutic properties.
  • Terpenoids are aromatic compounds found in many plants, also thought to hold medicinal properties.
  • Your cannabis can also be analysed for common contaminants such as pesticides, heavy metals, and mycotoxins.

When ready, your individual sample analysis results will be uploaded to the CAN-ACT Website.

You will need your CAN-ACT User ID and Password to view your results on the CAN-ACT Website.Please take note of your CAN-ACT User ID and Password. As the online survey is completely anonymised, there will be no way for the research team to remind you of your CAN-ACT User ID or reset your password.

To ensure your anonymity, please don’t tell anyone your CAN-ACT User ID or Password. Please also destroy any record of your CAN-ACT User D and Password once the study is over. This will ensure there is no way to link you with the responses you submit to the online survey.
