IV. Medicinal cannabis use
ACCESS TO MEDICINAL CANNABIS

The following questions ask about medicinal cannabis.

PRESCRIBED medicinal cannabis is prescribed by an Australian doctor and scripts are filled at a pharmacy or chemist.

NON-PRESCRIBED medicinal cannabis is not sourced from a doctor or pharmacy. Non-prescribed medicinal cannabis might be sourced through friends, family, online vendors, a dealer, or through self-cultivation.

Your responses are anonymous and confidential.
